Photos Android App
by Group 89
Jane Chang & Vladislav Tsoy

Home screen:
- Click an album to open
- Create new album
- Search photos for a tag

Upon opening an album:
- Delete the album
- Rename the album
- Add a photo
- Click on a photo to display it

Upon photo display:
- View photos in slideshow mode using Prev & Next buttons with tags displayed
- Move the photo to a different album
- Delete photo from album
- Add a tag
- Click on a tag in the list to prompt deletion
